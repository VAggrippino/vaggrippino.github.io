Resolutions Suck

19th December 2023 - Tuesday

New Year's Resolution A few ideas for 2024

Whether it's at the beginning of a year or at any other time, the concept of a resolution is ridiculous. You know you're gonna fail. Failure is built into the idea. It's just a question of when? It's become a meme. Don't do that to yourself.

Sure, some people keep their resolutions. But those people are abnormal.

Well, I don't know a solution, but I would like to do things a little differently in the near future.

So, here are a few ideas to help you head in the right direction...

Learn, Build, Teach

Learn

Pick a small topic from YouTube, an article, or social media and learn it.

Build

Create something that uses the new thing that you've learned.

Teach

In an article or video explain in detail what you built and how you built it.

Blog

This blog needs to be finished and made fully functional. Every article should be here and every YouTube video should be be associated with an article here. Even social media posts should mostly start their lives here.

YouTube

You like watching YouTube videos, don't you? Why not create them? It'll be fun. Don't worry about the quality of the videos. Don't worry about likes. Don't read the comments. Just do it for fun.
